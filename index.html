Libratrack System Overview Meet the researchers and learn how the platform works.
Smart Attendance + Library Monitoring

Libratrack is a simple and organized system for faster school record management.

Libratrack is a web-based attendance and library management system that helps schools organize student and teacher records in one place. It uses barcode scanning for faster attendance, simplifies book inventory and borrowing, and gives administrators an easy way to view records and reduce manual work.

Fast Barcode Scanning

Quickly scan IDs or barcodes to record attendance and open related information instantly.

Library Tracking

Monitor books, borrowing dates, return status, and overdue records with less manual checking.

Centralized Records

Keep student, teacher, attendance, and book data in one clean and accessible interface.
